## What is Sea Freight?

<!-- EN -->
Sea freight is the transportation of goods via cargo ships across oceans and waterways. It is the backbone of global trade, accounting for over 80% of the world's goods by volume. Whether you are moving raw materials, manufactured products, or consumer goods, sea freight offers the most cost-effective solution for large shipments.

There are two primary modes: **Full Container Load (FCL)** — where you rent an entire container — and **Less than Container Load (LCL)** — where your cargo shares space with other shippers. Choosing between them depends on your shipment volume, timeline, and budget.

<!-- ZH -->
海运是通过货船在海洋和水路上运输货物的方式。它是全球贸易的支柱，按体积计算占全球货物的80%以上。无论您运输的是原材料、制造产品还是消费品，海运都为大批量货物提供最具成本效益的解决方案。

主要有两种模式：**整箱运输（FCL）**——租用整个集装箱；**拼箱运输（LCL）**——您的货物与其他托运人共享空间。选择哪种方式取决于您的货物量、时间表和预算。

## How to Prepare Your Shipment

Before booking, ensure you have the following documents ready:

1. Commercial Invoice
2. Packing List
3. Bill of Lading (B/L)
4. Certificate of Origin (if required)
5. Customs Declaration forms

Proper documentation prevents delays at ports of entry and ensures smooth customs clearance. Our team provides full support at every step.
